# Presentation notes

## One-sentence pitch

FasalPramaan turns guided, geo-tagged crop evidence into an auditable human-review case, helping make field assessment more transparent and easier to follow.

## What to show

1. The README flowcharts: capture → verify → assist → review → explain.
2. Reviewer Command Centre: Overview, Map, Review Queue.
3. A human correction with a required reason and audit history.
4. The AI disclaimer and safe routing to review/inspection.

## What to say about the stack

Flutter capture app, Next.js reviewer dashboard, FastAPI evidence API, PostgreSQL/PostGIS, MinIO/S3-compatible evidence storage, Redis/Celery processing, and a replaceable AI service.

## What not to claim

- Automatic insurance approval or payout.
- Live PMFBY/YESTECH integration.
- Field-validated accuracy for every crop or damage type.
- Fraud-proof GPS.
